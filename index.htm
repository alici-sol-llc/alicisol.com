Alici

Alici (Alice V. de M. Sol) (born 28 January 1999) is a Brazilian-American singer, songwriter, and performer. Representing the cultural tapestry of Belo Horizonte, Brazil, Alici is a New York-based musician on a mission to join her birth country's serene sounds with the city's gritty energy.

She draws from her diverse upbringing split between Brazil, Seattle, and the UK to redefine the idea of what Bossa Nova and Trip Hop sounds like to a contemporary audience. Inspired by timeless acts such as Gal Costa, J Dilla, Sade, and Portishead, Alici stands as a beacon of creativity, passion, and innovation, breathing new life into Brazilian-inspired R&B and jazz.

In 2021, she received a Bachelor of Music in Songwriting from the Berklee College of Music in Boston, MA.
